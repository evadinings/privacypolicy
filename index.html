Eva Dinings Privacy Policy

Introduction

Eva Dinings ("we," "us," or "our") is a platform designed to connect catering staff with job opportunities. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled securely and responsibly. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect the information of our users ("you" or "staff") when you use the Eva Dinings app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

This includes data you provide when you sign up or create an account on Eva Dinings, such as:

  • Name
  • Contact information (e.g., email address, phone number)
  • Address
  • Date of birth
  • Profile photo

1.2 Work and Employment Information

Data relevant to your professional profile, including:

  • Job preferences
  • Skills and experience
  • Work history
  • Certifications and qualifications

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to connect you with job opportunities and to enhance your experience on Eva Dinings, including:

  • Facilitating job matches based on your skills and preferences
  • Communicating with you about your account and job applications
  • Improving our platform through user feedback and data analysis

3. Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary to provide the Eva Dinings service or as required by law.

4. Security of Your Information

We take appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
